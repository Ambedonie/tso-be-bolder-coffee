About Tso-Be-Bolder Coffee

A local coffee concept built around bravery, belonging, and that perfect first sip.

Our shop started with a simple idea: coffee should do more than wake you up — it should remind you to keep going, keep dreaming, and keep being bolder than yesterday.

We focus on small-batch beans, thoughtful flavor profiles, and a menu that feels cozy, approachable, and just a little bit adventurous.

Whether you're popping in between classes, working remotely, or catching up with friends, we’ve got a seat and a cup waiting for you.

What we care about:

• Ethically sourced beans
• Inclusive, welcoming space
• Craft drinks without the pretension
• Supporting local makers & events
